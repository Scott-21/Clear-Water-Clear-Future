Our Advocacy

Our mission is to support Sustainable Development Goal 6: ensuring access to clean water and sanitation for all. We believe that water is not just a necessity—it is a right.

The Problem

According to the World Health Organization, 2.2 billion people worldwide do not have safe drinking water. In the Philippines, 1 in 10 families still struggles to access clean water, and 1 in 5 lacks basic sanitation facilities. This crisis leads to diseases such as diarrhea, cholera, and typhoid, which affect millions of children.

What We Do

Our website provides information, resources, and ways to help. From spreading awareness to supporting water projects, we encourage everyone to take part in making a change.
